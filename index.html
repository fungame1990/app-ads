About the Game

Maze It is a story-driven puzzle adventure that blends strategic thinking with thrilling survival challenges. Step into the shoes of Bryan, a man wrongly accused and on the run. Trapped inside a mysterious maze complex, he must navigate deadly traps, uncover clues, and prove his innocence before it’s too late. Choose between Time Attack and Survival modes, each offering a unique test of skill. In Time Attack, race the clock through immersive mazes in either 3D or 2.5D perspectives. In Survival, every step is a fight to stay alive. Maze It offers a gripping journey where every choice could be your last. Can you help Bryan escape the maze and uncover the truth?
